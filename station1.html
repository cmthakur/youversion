Listen to the audio and fill in the timestamp for corresponding verse. You can use hot keys during fill in the time stamp.

Hot keys are:

Play/pause => Ctrl + /

Rewind => Ctrl + <

Fast_forward => Ctr + >

Stop => Ctr + s

Insert timestamp => Ctr + i

Click on Heart to sanap the time snaps.

Fast forward/backward will fast forward and backward 3 seconds respectively.

After filling up all the timestamp press "I'm Done!" button to submit your task.
