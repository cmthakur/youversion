Listen to the audio and transcribe the audio. You can use hot keys during transcription the audio

Hot keys are:

Play/pause => Ctrl + /

Rewind => Ctrl + <

Fast_forward => Ctr + >

Stop => Ctr + s

When toggle play media, it will go 3 seconds back ,for Rewind it will move 3 sec back and for forward it will move 3 sec forward

After completing the transcription for the audio, click submit button to submit your final transcription.

Click spell check to check the spelling for your transcribed words.
